Seattle Heels Camp 2026

Seattle, Washington's first-ever heels dance convention—an immersive two-day experience created to celebrate femininity, strength, and confidence through movement.

Featuring 8 workshops led by renowned heels instructors from across the country, each class offers a 90-minute heels choreography that dives deep into musicality, balance, control, and performance quality. Designed to be accessible and empowering for dancers of all levels, this convention welcomes everyone—from first-time heels dancers to seasoned performers.

The event spans two full days, with four workshops each day, giving dancers the opportunity to train intensively while fully embodying the art of heels dance. Beyond choreography, Seattle Heels Camp focuses on building balance, activating the muscles essential for heels work, and embracing femininity with power and intention.

Come train, connect, and step into your confidence—this is more than a convention, it's an empowered heels experience.
